EPC of Solar Ground Mounted Projects and Rooftop Projects

We undertake outsourced EPC projects for Rooftop & Ground Mounted Solar Solutions. We take full responsibility for the execution of project from concept to commissioning. As a client, you get to directly meet our supplies and sub-vendors for a fair and transparent discussion on the pricing of components. Clients release the POs to vendors directly, without any markups! However, we always take full responsibility for the quality and timelines. Our engineers and supervisors at are site 24x7. All supervision, QC and project monitoring would be under the scope of ANMOL and client does not have to spend any extra money to execute the project. We only charge a fixed Project Management Fees to cover our overheads and margin.

Project Management Services for Solar Ground Mount Projects

For clients who are not inclined to outsource EPC services for a solar project, ANMOL will manage the end to end project monitoring and control, thru its PMS services, for a fix fee to cover its overheads and margin.

Trading of Solar Modules and Inverters

ANMOL operates a Trading arm of its own. Here we stock and re-sell modules and investers of multiple brands like Adani, ABB and Solis to name a few. We always pass on the price advantages of our trading arm to our EPC clients. As a result, our customers always spent much lesser than market rates to own their solar projects.
