WHO WE ARE

ANMOL is in the business of “Unlocking The Power Of Nature” and making it accessible to more people.

A premium renewable energy developing company, with offices in Hyderabad, Bengaluru and NCR, ANMOL engages in leasing solar power plants and helps in creating,installing and maintaining them.
Founded by an Industry Veteran Mr. Anupam Mathur who has extensive experience of working across the globe, primarily in countries like Singapore, Thailand, UK, North and South America and India, ANMOL operates on his vision as a dedicated agency that provides optimum EPC solutions to ground mounted projects through its unique open-book concept to clients who need to commission up to 25MW solar projects.

Our Mission

As a renewable energy sourcing business, our mission is to eradicate the dependence on high costing thermal energy and replace it with an eco-friendly, efficacious measure by implanting a net-metered electricity generating system.

Our Vision

Vision - Our Vision is to provide meaningful capitalization and customized solutions for the ever-increasing electricity costs based on client needs. We want to be a preferred player in this space locally and eventually on a global scale

Founder

With an experience of over 20+ years in the business of renewable energy powers, the Founder and CEO,
Mr. Anupam Mathur has had a distinguished career leading ultra-large projects for Mytrah Energy, Adani Power, Suzlon, Inox and NSL. Hailing from Ghaziabad (Uttar Pradesh, India), he is an alumni of DAV college, Chandigarh and holds a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering from Punjab Engineering College.
