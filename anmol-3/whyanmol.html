About Us

Unmatched Price
Open Book Policy

Our unequivocal commitment to all our customers is to commission solar projects with top quality and best-in-class components at a cost unmatched by any other vendor. As a client, you get to directly meet our supplies and sub-vendors for a fair and transparent discussion on the pricing of components. Clients release the POs to vendors directly, without any markups!

However, we always take full responsibility for the quality and timelines. Our engineers and supervisors at are site 24x7. This unique open book policy reduces your CapEx by a big margin, without compromising on quality.

Flexible Schemes

ANMOL provides two different schemes, both of which help in creating a pool of savings in the long run. One requires zero investment and a minimal monthly bill creating 20% savings. The other requires one-time investment and no bills creating 80% savings. As a client you have the flexibility to choose what you want based on your requirement.

Domain Expertise

Renewable energy projects need domain specific expertise to be executed properly. Anmol management team brings in this expertise which they have gained through years of extensive industry exposure.
ANMOL Founder, Mr Anupam Mathur has hands on experience in executing large solar projects. He has commissioned more than 1000 MW of solar projects across several locations in India. He was formerly the Head of Renewable Energy of Adani Power and Chief Procurement Officer & Chief of Projects for Mytrah Energy Ltd before starting AGEPL. Mr N K Gupta, AGEPL Founding Partner is a seasoned Project Management professional with a Civil Engineering background. He has 27 years of extensive experience leading and
executing large EPC contracts.

Reliability &
Financial Stability

ANMOL is backed by a group of leading investors from the industry who take financial positions in our projects. Therefore, we never face any cashflow issues, which is a common bottleneck faced by most solar EPC contractors.
