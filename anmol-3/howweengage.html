About Us

ANMOL is in the business of “Unlocking The Power Of Nature” and making it accessible to more people. A premium renewable energy developing company, with offices in Hyderabad, Bengaluru and NCR .

HOW WE ENGAGE

We engage with our clients in a very fair and transparent manner through our flexible schemes and open book policy that provides unmatched and competitive price advantage.

It allows them to save significantly on Cap-ex for EPC Solutions.

We follow a meticulous 6 step process of engagement
with our clients as outlined below

Detailed Survey

We start with a detailed survey of the facility and the client’s requirement. Based on that we prepare plan of action and share with client.

Financial Information

We are a financially stable organization with good credit rating and promotor track record. Client can access our two year audited balance sheets and bank
statements if required.

Letter of Intent

We then seek a letter of intent from client expressing interest to switch to solar energy.

Technical Due Diligence

During this step we look at past 12 months electricity bills of client. We then prepare a system design followed by a technical and legal proposal.

Detailed Proposal & Power Purchase Agreement

Post technical due diligence, we prepare proposal and power purchase agreement.

Project Sign-Off

Project construction and commissioning starts. Our team will work diligently and take complete responsibility to deliver the project with quality and within the agreed timelines.
